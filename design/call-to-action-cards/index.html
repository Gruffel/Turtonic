Call to Action Cards

Call to Action Cards

Project Description

In this project I tried to focus on making a feature that would highlight a call to action. In this specific and initial use case it is for a student looking to contact their advisor.

Scheduling Link:

Previously the Advisors information was tucked into an aside tag with no styling other than the aside containers styles. The details of each advisor with their images drowened out the information of how to contact them.

I focused on seperating the information into two sections that were quick to read and gave room to breathe.

The first section is the information about the Advisor and the relation to what they advise. Making the first few lines informative about if this advisor is the reader's advisor or not.

Next I added the link to the scheduling systemfor that advisor appear over their image. This was planned for 3 reasons, the first is to draw the eye of the reader to the link by using faces. Readers eyes are naturally drawen to faces. The second reason is to reinforce the quick scan of 'is this my advisor?' for any student that has met with their advisor before but may not remember their name. Lastly this also reinforces the idea the students are meeting with a real person who is present and engaged with them.

The last section is more of the additional informaiton if they have quick questions or need to contact them immediately.

Lastly with the impact of COVID-19 I added a link section for the virtual meetings. This helped retain clear and quick access virtually to what would have been an impromtu in person meeting at their office.
